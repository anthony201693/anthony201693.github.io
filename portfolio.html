Pew Pew Space Edition

Pew pew space edition is a game where you become one with a spaceship defending
your life against a range of monsters. You have to survive as long as you can
and kill all the enemies that are in your way.

The scoring system that was in the game was the ammount to points you would gain by killing your enemies.
The randomness that was in the game is how the enemies would fall down the screen randomly.
The game gets harder over time due to bosses start spawning and the enemies spawn faster.
This game is like a survival game; where people have to survire and keep killing. But there you can lose due to your three life points.
